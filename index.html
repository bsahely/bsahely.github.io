I'm a Senior Technical Business Analyst and RevOps Product Owner with 11+ years of experience working where enterprise complexity lives — SaaS, Fintech, Software Products, Telco — industries where the margin for ambiguity isn't just low, it's professionally dangerous.

My favourite kind of problem? The one nobody else wants to touch. I've spent a Tuesday night at 2am untangling a 290+ invoice sync failure between Salesforce and a Billing platform — not because I was asked to, but because I needed to understand why it broke, not just that it did. That instinct — to go deeper, pull the thread, follow the data — is what separates a discovery session from a real diagnosis.

I live inside the Salesforce ecosystem: Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, CPQ, Agentforce. But living in it means more than knowing the landscape — I configure CPQ product rules and pricing logic myself, debug and fix broken Flows without waiting on a developer, stand up Gong Playbooks from scratch, and run the kind of M&A discovery work that lets a newly acquired business start selling/renewing through existing processes on day 30. I don't just document what needs to be built. I help build it.

Most recently, I led Agentic AI implementations that generated ~$1M in influenced pipeline and contributed to ~$1.5M in churn reduction. Great BA/PO work doesn't support the business — it moves the needle.

Based in Bengaluru. Novice swimmer. Committed binge-watcher. Serious about flat whites. I believe the best product thinking happens in real conversations — not in decks, not in standups, and almost never in a requirements document.
