I'm a Senior Technical Business Analyst and Product Owner with 11+ years of experience driving enterprise transformation across SaaS, Fintech, and Telco — where the problems are complex, the stakeholders are many, and the margin for ambiguity is zero.

My work sits deep in the Salesforce ecosystem — across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, CPQ, and Agentforce — but my real expertise is translating messy business realities into clean, shippable requirements. I've led Q2C process redesigns, spearheaded company-wide AI initiatives, and orchestrated CRM migrations across global teams at companies like Quickbase, VMware, and Accenture — working with clients including Nokia and Adecco.

Most recently, I led Agentic AI implementations that generated ~$1M in influenced pipeline and contributed to ~$1.5M in churn reduction — proof that great BA/PO work doesn't just support the business, it moves the needle.

I believe the best analysts are invisible. When I've done my job well, engineers ship with confidence, stakeholders trust the plan, and users actually get value. That's the bar I hold myself to.

Outside of work, I'm based in Bengaluru — and I believe the best product thinking happens in conversations, not documents.
